    About Vanguard Components

    Vanguard Components is a high-growth gaming and PC parts company focused on premium GPUs, CPUs, motherboards, and custom builds for enthusiasts and esports teams. Founded 14 months ago, the company runs a lean team of 36 employees and has achieved strong margins through direct-to-consumer sales, B2B partnerships, and a subscription-based warranty & tuning service.

    • Core products: GPUs, CPUs, motherboards, custom builds, cooling solutions
    • Channels: eCommerce, wholesale, enterprise gaming rigs
    • Competitive edge: Rapid fulfillment, curated high-margin bundles, premium support
